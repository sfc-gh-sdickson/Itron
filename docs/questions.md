# Itron Intelligence Agent - Test Questions

## Environmental / Emissions

1. What was our total Scope 1 and 2 GHG emissions reduction from the 2019 baseline last quarter?
2. Show the trend in customer avoided emissions over the past 3 years.
3. What is our carbon intensity per million dollars of revenue and is it on track for the 2035 target?
4. Break down our Scope 3 emissions by category and identify which category has the largest year-over-year increase.
5. How do our absolute emissions compare to our approved Science Based Target trajectory?
6. What percentage of our electricity consumption comes from renewable sources and how has that changed since 2020?

## Water Operations

7. Which water meters in the Northwest region show the highest leak probability?
8. How much non-revenue water have we reduced since deploying smart meters?
9. What is the current distribution system water loss rate by district and which districts exceed the 10% threshold?
10. How many DI-enabled meters do we have deployed by region?
11. Show the correlation between smart meter deployment density and non-revenue water reduction across all service territories.
12. Which water distribution zones have experienced pressure anomalies in the last 30 days and what is the estimated volume loss?

## Energy / Grid

13. Predict energy demand for the Southwest region for the next 14 days.
14. Which grid assets have a failure probability above 70% and are overdue for inspection?
15. What is the average outage duration by region and how does it compare to the prior year?
16. How many distributed energy resources are currently connected to our managed grid segments and what is their total capacity?
17. What is the peak demand reduction attributable to our demand response programs this summer compared to last summer?
18. Identify the top 10 transformers by loading percentage that are approaching end-of-life and rank them by criticality score.

## Social / Workforce

19. How does our TRIR compare to industry benchmarks and what SDG does it align with?
20. What is our employee voluntary turnover rate by department and how does it compare to our retention target?
21. What percentage of our workforce completed safety training in the last 12 months and which regions are below the 95% threshold?
22. Show the trend in workforce diversity metrics across leadership levels over the past 5 years.

## Governance

23. What percentage of our board is independent and how does that compare to our governance target?
24. How many critical cybersecurity incidents have we had this fiscal year and what is the mean time to resolution?
25. List all ESG-related policies that are due for review in the next 90 days and their current approval status.
26. What is our supplier code of conduct compliance rate and how many suppliers have been flagged for non-conformance?

## SDG Alignment

27. What is our waste diversion rate and which SDG target does it support?
28. Map our top 10 ESG KPIs to their corresponding UN Sustainable Development Goals and show progress against each.
29. Which of our operational programs contribute to SDG 6 (Clean Water and Sanitation) and what measurable impact have they delivered this year?
30. How do our climate-related disclosures align with TCFD recommendations and which SDG 13 targets do they address?

## Predictions / Forecasts

31. Based on current trajectory, will we meet our 2030 Scope 1 and 2 reduction target of 50%?
32. Forecast our water savings impact for the next fiscal year assuming current smart meter deployment rates continue.
33. What is the predicted failure rate for our AMI network infrastructure over the next 24 months and what capex is needed to maintain 99.9% uptime?

## Cross-Domain

34. Show the relationship between our grid modernization investments and the corresponding reduction in carbon emissions per MWh delivered.
35. How do our water conservation programs and energy efficiency initiatives jointly contribute to community resilience metrics?
36. Correlate employee safety incident rates with asset inspection compliance to identify operational risk hotspots.
37. What is the combined avoided emissions impact across all product lines (water, gas, electric) for our top 5 customers by revenue?

## Ontology Validation

38. Validate the ESG classification of "Total Recordable Incident Rate" using the ontology.
39. Confirm the correct GRI disclosure reference for our water withdrawal metric and verify it maps to the expected SDG target.
40. Check whether "Customer Avoided Emissions" is classified under Scope 1, 2, or 3 in our ESG ontology and explain the rationale.
41. List all metrics in the ontology tagged as "Environmental" that do not yet have a corresponding data source mapped in the semantic model.
42. Validate that our governance metrics taxonomy is consistent with SASB Infrastructure sector standards.
